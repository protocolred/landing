Protocol Privacy Policy

Welcome to Protocol! We're committed to respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our product.

We collect minimal data necessary for the functioning of our product, including your IP address and device data. This data is used solely to identify users and does not include personal details such as names, email addresses, or physical addresses.

Information is automatically collected from your device when you start using Protocol.

We collect this data to enhance your experience, for user identification, and to troubleshoot any issues that might arise.

Your security is important to us. We use secure transport and device authentication to help ensure your data is transmitted safely. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

User Control over Data

You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time. If you wish to do so, please email us at contact@protocol.red

Your use of Protocol constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use.
